Who Am I?

Hello, I’m Mackenzie Miller, a college student dedicated to mastering the art of visual communication. Currently, I’m pursuing a Digital Media degree at Penn State Behrend, and I’m set to graduate in 2026.

Academic Journey

Graphic design wasn’t always my path. I came into college planning to become a math teacher. Why did I switch? In highschool, I took two years of graphic design, it was the only class I looked forward to everyday. After graduating, I was using what I learned to do projects for my employer at the time. I had been at the job for 3 years, and they wanted to use my knowledge in both the job and graphic design, to create a training binder for the new employees.

The whole time I was disliking my major, and wanted to switch to something more creative. I spent so much time researching, hoping I could find something that I would actually enjoy. One day, a coworker at the time had asked me if graphic design was my major, and something clicked in my brain, telling me that's exactly what I needed to do.

Notable Projects

I get asked to do little projects for people here and there, and I love to do it. Most notably though, I do a lot of the graphic design projects for the Sigma Tau Gamma fraternity at Behrend, creating event posters and such. I am also currently in a digital media class, learning how to more of the technological side of the subject. I’ve created my own video game, and even the website you’re currently on.

Future Goals

Looking ahead, I'm excited about the possibilities that lie ahead in my career. My goal is to become a freelance graphic designer, but am also willing to work for a company. I believe that the knowledge and skills I'm gaining in college will prepare me to make a meaningful impact in the field of Digital Media.

Personal Interests

When I'm not hitting the books, you can often find me outside, or doing some sort of art. I love nature and things like painting and drawing. These activities help me recharge and bring a unique perspective to my studies.
